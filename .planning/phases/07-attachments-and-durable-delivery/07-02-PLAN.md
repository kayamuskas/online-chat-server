<objective>
Add watermark-based durable delivery catch-up to the existing messaging engine.

Purpose: When a client reconnects after being offline, it sends its last known watermark and receives only missed messages (D-52..D-54). This satisfies MSG-06 (persistent delivery after reconnect) and MSG-09 (no secondary unbounded queue) because the messages table IS the delivery mechanism.
Output: Extended MessageHistoryQuery, repository, and controller to support `after_watermark` parameter.
</objective>
